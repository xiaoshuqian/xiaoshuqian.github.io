Detailed outline

  • NP (Levtin)
    • Tractable (polynomial time) vs intractable problem
    • Decision problem
    • Decidable vs in-decidable
    • A nondeterministic algorithm is a two-stage procedure that takes as its input an instance I of a decision problem and solve it with a nondeterministic ('guessing') stage and deterministic ('verification') stage
    • NP: Non-deterministic Polynomial
    • NP-hard: with a lower bound: It's at least as hard as the hardest problem in NP-class
    • NP-complete: With a lower and upper bound: It's in NP-class and is NP-H
