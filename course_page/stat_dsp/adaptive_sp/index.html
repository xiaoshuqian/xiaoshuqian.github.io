Description

This is an introductory course to the theory and applications of adaptive filters. Topics covered include Wiener filter, steepest gradient algorithm, LMS (Least Mean Square) algorithm, normalized LMS algorithm and RLS (Recursive Least Square) algorithm. We will also describe some of the applications of adaptive filters in the area of digital communication, data compression and system identification. Students will be encouraged to select one of application areas and conduct a more in-depth study in their area of choice.
