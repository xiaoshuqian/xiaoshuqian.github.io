Oct 4 -- Oct 6 to Napa Valley with Lei and Kun

  • Brief itinerary
    • Fri Oct 4: Depart San Jose at ~2pm and arrive in Meritage Resort (near Grape Crusher Statue) around 4pm (Took us about 2 hours while driving without traffic takes ~1:20). Dinner at a chinese Restaurant in downtown Napa Valley.
    • Sat Oct 5: Went to three wine tasting sites, dinner at resort (the last two places are close together about 45 min drive from Meritage, the first one is about 30min drive):
      • V. Sattui, a place for wine tasting and food (no tickets needed)
      • Castello di Amorosa, tour of the castle & tastings of Italian-inspired wines. (tickets needed)
      • Sterling Vineyards, taking dongle to the wine tasting area (tickets needed)
    • Sun Oct 6: Drove back and visited Tilden Regional Park near Berkeley. Walked on Seaview Trail and then to Vollmer Peak, where antennas originally built during WW2 could be seen. Vollmer Peak is a good place to view from distance Golden Gate bridge, EBMUD's San Pablo & Briones Reservoirs, San Francisco Bay, etc
